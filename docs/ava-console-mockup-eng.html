How does APQP connect to IATF 16949?
APQP is the planning method; IATF 16949 is the management-system standard that requires planning of that kind. Clause 8.3 (Design & Development) expects a structured product-quality planning process — APQP's five phases are the industry's accepted way to meet it — and the outputs IATF auditors look for map directly to APQP deliverables: the Control Plan (Annex A), FMEAs from phases 2–3, and PPAP as the evidence of clause 8.6 product approval. In practice: run APQP, and you generate the records an IATF 16949 audit asks for. APQP 2nd Ed §1 IATF 16949 cl. 8.3, 8.6 Control Plan Annex A
